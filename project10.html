Case Closed

Case Closed is an interactive investigative experience that blends storytelling with user-centered design. The project challenges users to engage with a digital crime scene by analyzing clues, interpreting visual information, and making connections to solve a narrative-driven mystery. Designed with a focus on usability and engagement, the experience emphasizes intuitive interaction, clear visual hierarchy, and progressive information discovery. Through this project, I explored how interface design and narrative structure can work together to guide user behavior, encourage critical thinking, and create a sense of immersion..
