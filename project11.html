The Challenge

Building empathy through experience

Many people struggle to understand the daily experiences of individuals with ADHD and dyslexia. The goal of Magidemia was to create an engaging experience that encourages empathy through gameplay rather than explanation alone.

The Goal

What we set out to do

  • Increase awareness of learning disabilities.
  • Create an accessible educational experience.
  • Use gameplay mechanics to simulate real challenges.
  • Make learning engaging and approachable.

My Role

What I contributed

  • Created all character sprites and animations.
  • Developed the game's visual identity and art direction.
  • Designed the user interface and menus.
  • Contributed to gameplay design and player experience.
  • Collaborated on development and implementation.

Research & Inspiration

Understanding the problem space

We grounded the project in research on ADHD and dyslexia to understand how these conditions affect learning, attention, and information processing. Educational games and accessibility-first design served as key inspiration for how we structured the gameplay experience.

1 in 10 school-age students have ADHD
1 in 20 school-age students have dyslexia
20–25% of students have learned about disabilities in class
