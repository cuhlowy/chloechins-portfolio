Magidemia

Many struggle to understand the reality of ADHD and dyslexia, as traditional explanations often fail to build true empathy. Magidemia bridges this gap through an interactive web game that simulates these learning disabilities. By experiencing these challenges firsthand in an immersive environment, players gain a deeper, more personal understanding than passive learning could ever provide.
