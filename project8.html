Sound Into Form

This mixed media piece translates the first minute of the song into a visual experience. Using gouache, thread, silver foil, paint markers, and pencil, I aimed to capture the mood, rhythm, and layered textures of the music. Each material reflects a different sonic element—from shimmering highs to grounded bass—creating a tactile and atmospheric response to sound.
