About the Organization

Art Against Overdose

Art Against Overdose is a community initiative dedicated to raising awareness about the opioid crisis through creativity, education, and public engagement. By bringing together artists, advocates, and community members, the organization uses art as a platform to reduce stigma, encourage conversation, and support overdose prevention efforts.

Our class partnered with the organization to organize a student-led art market that raised funds while promoting awareness of the impact of substance use disorder. Through this collaboration, we explored how design and community events can inspire dialogue and create meaningful social impact.

Why This Project Mattered

Design has the power to do more than communicate — it can build community, spark conversations, and support meaningful change. This project challenged us to use creativity as a tool for advocacy and fundraising in partnership with Art Against Overdose.

The Challenge

What we were up against

How might we organize a collaborative fundraising event that effectively communicates the mission of Art Against Overdose while creating an engaging experience for both artists and attendees?

Reflection

What I took away

Collaborating with my classmates, community partners, and the LSU community reinforced my passion for using design to create experiences that make a positive impact.
