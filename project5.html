Beetle on the Road!

This energetic illustration captures a beetle mid-journey, bold against a vibrant background. Inspired by movement and street-style poster art, the piece uses expressive text, saturated colors, and a central beetle figure to create a sense of action and personality. It’s a small creature with a big presence — ready to take on the road.
